The WNBA Has Its Own 3‑Point Revolution

The NBA is in the midst of a much discussed three-point revolution. Teams now have multiple three-point threats on the court at once, changing the style of play by stretching defenses and forgoing mid-range two-point shots. My question was simple: Is the WNBA experiencing its own three-point revolution?
