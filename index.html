About Me

Hi, I'm Morgan. I'm a Portland-based data visualization designer with a background in electrical engineering and technical writing. My journey to data visualization was not direct, but I have found it to be a perfect blend of my talents and passions. I love distilling complex data and information into a comprehensive, engaging, and visually pleasing product.

I am an extremely quick learner and love to develop new skills while volunteering for causes I care about.

I'm an amateur photographer, board game player, sports fan, world traveler, and mom.
